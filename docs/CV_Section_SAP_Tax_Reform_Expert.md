# SAP Functional Expert – Brazil Tax Reform (Reforma Tributária)

**Role Overview**
Acted as the lead SAP Functional Expert throughout all project phases (Discover, Prepare, Explore, Realize, Deploy, and Run) for the implementation of the Brazilian Tax Reform (Constitutional Amendment EC 132/2023 and Complementary Law LC 214/2025). Spearheaded the strategic alignment of complex business processes with the new dual VAT system, ensuring seamless integration of the Contribuição sobre Bens e Serviços (CBS), Imposto sobre Bens e Serviços (IBS), and Imposto Seletivo (IS) into the SAP landscape. Facilitated cross-functional collaboration among IT, Finance, Tax departments, and external partners to guarantee regulatory compliance, system stability, and operational continuity during the critical transition period.

**Key Responsibilities & Achievements**

**End-to-End Project Lifecycle Management**
Directed the end-to-end SAP implementation lifecycle using the SAP Activate methodology. Conducted comprehensive impact assessments during the Explore phase to map the effects of the new tax regime on existing Finance (FI), Sales and Distribution (SD), and Materials Management (MM) processes. Orchestrated the Realize phase by configuring core system components, and managed the Deploy phase by preparing cutover strategies and go-live readiness plans. Provided steady-state production support (Hypercare) to ensure system stabilization post-deployment.

**Business Process Alignment & System Configuration**
Led the technical transformation required to transition from origin-based taxation to the destination principle. Configured complex tax structures, including new tax codes for CBS, IBS, and IS, and updated pricing procedures (TAXBRA/RVABRA) to support tax-exclusive base calculations ("por fora"). Adapted master data and Brazilian Localization layers to accommodate the new rate management system, encompassing standard, reduced (40% and 60%), and zero-rated regimes. Implemented full non-cumulativity logic to ensure accurate credit appropriation across the production chain.

**Regulatory Compliance & Electronic Invoicing (NF-e)**
Ensured continuous alignment with evolving legislative updates and SEFAZ technical notes. Managed the implementation of critical SAP Notes (e.g., 3561376, 3552901, 3552903, 3556404, 3569061) to maintain system resilience against regulatory changes. Oversaw the adaptation of electronic invoice (NF-e) layouts and Document and Reporting Compliance (DRC) requirements, incorporating mandatory new fields and fiscal validations. Prepared the system for advanced mechanisms such as the proposed split payment model for CBS.

**Cross-Functional Collaboration & Testing**
Served as the primary liaison between technical teams and business stakeholders. Collaborated closely with Finance and Tax departments to translate complex fiscal requirements into actionable SAP configurations. Partnered with external consultants and vendors to integrate third-party tax solutions and ensure holistic compliance. Designed and executed exhaustive Functional Unit Tests (FUT), User Acceptance Tests (UAT), and regression cycles covering all transactional flows, import/export processes, and special regimes to validate system accuracy and stability prior to go-live.
